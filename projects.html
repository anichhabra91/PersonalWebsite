Selected Projects

  1. Modeling Financial Market Sentiments to Predict Price Movement (Cornell University): Developed a pipeline combining BERT-based news sentiment analysis with technical indicators (RSI, EMA, MACD) and improved accuracy by 17% through feature selection and hyperparameter tuning.
  2. Derivative Pricing and Implied Volatility Modelling (Cornell University): Implemented a Black–Scholes and Monte Carlo framework, and trained Random Forest regressors to achieve an R² of 93% on implied volatility predictions.
  3. Quantitative Risk Optimization (Cornell University): Engineered a minimum variance portfolio using Mean–Variance Optimization, yielding an annualized Sharpe ratio of 0.656, and conducted VaR and ES analyses for downside risk mitigation.
  4. Sports Analysis (Deutsche Sporthochschule Köln, Germany): Built a classification model with PCA and RFE to identify top players; applied Apriori algorithms to analyze cricket matchups and support data-driven team selection.
  5. Application of Queuing Theory in Outpatient Departments (BITS Pilani): Formulated deterministic and probabilistic queuing models and simulated patient flow using Monte Carlo methods to reduce wait times and optimize server allocation.
  6. BITSMart — E‑Commerce Web Application (BITS Pilani): Designed wallet transactions and report generation features; implemented role-based authentication for admins, managers, and users to enhance security and functionality.
  7. Cost Optimization in Various Fields (BITS Pilani): Analyzed coalition impacts in newspaper supply problems and evaluated solar PV investment strategies under stochastic demand to recommend optimal vendor pricing and system sizing.
  8. Loan Prediction App (BITS Pilani): Developed and deployed a logistic regression model achieving 82.92% accuracy within a real-time web app, streamlining loan approval decisions.
